We appreciate our suppliers and partners

We are currently working with over 600 suppliers and receive produce from over 14,000 farmers drawn from across the country. Through this relationship, the local farmers that supply their fresh produce to our stores across the country are assured of a ready and reliable market which translates to a steady income. We are also pleased to currently have over 40,000 SKUs stocked on our shelves. This is made possible by the many different suppliers who equally believe in our journey to becoming Marsabits number one retailer. This has enabled us to fulfill our customers’ need for variety and even exceed their expectations
