Our Product and Services

Bakery

Our in-house bakeries make a selection of fresh bread, pastries and cakes everyday. We also take custom orders for parties and catering events. If you wish to do some baking of your own, we have a large selection of icing tools, baking trays, silicon sheets, cookie cutters and decoration.

Coffee

Relax and enjoy a coffee and gelato (made fresh and in-house) at any of our locations. You can also pick up a delicious pizza at our Slipway location in Marsabit.

Florist

Visit our resident florist for a beautiful selection of flowers and vases.
